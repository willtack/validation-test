**Instructions**

Simply scroll through this web page to get a feel for the heatmaps (shuffled and unlabelled). Each map thresholded at a w-score of 1.5 (essentially one and a half standard deviations). The maximum w-score is 4. The color map is red-yellow (the range is displayed in the bottom left corner). The hemisphere orientation is left=left, as displayed in the first image.
